Philippines

Ideal for diving: warm water, good visibility and a varied underwater world. There are luxurious coral reefs everywhere, surrounded by numerous species of marine life - including endemics. And also sharks, turtles, barracudas, tuna. On one of the more than 7,000 Philippine islands, both experienced divers and beginner amateurs will find something to do.
